When you fill bored, you can try this addictive game out. Despite its simplicity, it can be really fun and relaxing. An all time classic, Snake will challenge your reflexes to the maximum. In this iteration, you can also compete locally with your friends, to see who is the best Snake player. Additionally, each point you aquire in a game gives you a certain amount of in-game currency, with which you can buy colors to personalize your snake.
